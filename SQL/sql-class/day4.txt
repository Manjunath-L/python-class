21. WQTD FNAME,LNAME,JOB AND SAL, IF THE EMP NOT WORKING AS SALESMAN,MANAGER
   ,DEELOPER (WITHOUT OUT USING THE SPECIAL OPERATOR)

FROM EMP
WHERE JOB!='SALESMAN' AND JOB!='DEVELOPER' AND JOB!='MANAGER';

OR

        IT WILL TAKE SINGLE VALUE IN THE LHS AND MULTIPLE VALUES AT RHS.
